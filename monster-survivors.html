Monster Survivors

Battle endless waves of monsters and prove your mettle in this thrilling survival game! Can you withstand the onslaught?

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an action-packed roguelike survival game where you face off against increasingly challenging waves of monsters. As you defeat enemies, you'll gain experience, level up, and unlock new abilities and upgrades to help you survive longer.

Key Features:

  • Intense, fast-paced combat against hordes of unique monsters.
  • A wide array of powerful abilities and upgrades to choose from.
  • Roguelike progression: each run is different!
  • Challenging boss battles to test your skills.
  • Simple controls, deep gameplay.

How to Play

Navigating the treacherous world of Monster Survivors is straightforward:

  • Movement: Use your mouse to guide your character or WASD/Arrow keys (if supported by the game).
  • Attacking: Your character typically attacks automatically. Focus on positioning and dodging enemy attacks.
  • Upgrades: Collect experience gems dropped by defeated monsters to level up. Choose from a selection of random upgrades to enhance your abilities.
  • Objective: Survive for as long as you can against the relentless monster waves!
